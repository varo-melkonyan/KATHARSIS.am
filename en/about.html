WHAT IS GRAPHIC DESIGN
WHY IS IT IMPORTANT

Graphic design is a way of visual communication through typography, photography, and illustrations. It is a type of visual language that helps people to solve problems in a creative way. Graphic design has considerably developed for the past two decades. The demand for graphic designs has grown so much that it is almost impossible to find a business which doesn’t use graphic design or elements of graphic design. At present, graphic design is highly necessary and irreplaceable in every field. Graphic design helps businesses to earn more credibility among customers, it is an essential part of marketing, and increases the business profits.

SERVICES
WE PROVIDE

Our job is one of the best and the most creative jobs out there. We undertake large-scale projects, create the impossible and deliver them to you. We provide the following services:

Designing a company style
Creating, building a logo and its application, picking colors, color control, color balance, spacing, picking a font, creating elements.

Print documents of business design
Business cards, flyers, booklets, templates, envelopes, file folders, notebooks, ID badges, and others documents upon request.

Designing marketing materials
T-shirts, caps, pens, cups, USB flash drives and other items upon request.

Website design
Online shops, news, informational, entertainment, commercial websites and applications (considering the latest technological developments we can create 3 different versions for your website: for computer desktop, for tablets, and mobile).

Poster design
Social, commercial, formative banners, set of posters, scrolling and motion banners and billboards.

3D design
Modeling and rendering of interior, exterior, apartment buildings, video game and movie characters and environments, visual effects, and commercial items.

NAMING
AND LOGO

KATHARSIS
The name of our brand comes from Latin and doesn’t have its translation in many languages. The word KATHARSIS is mostly used in the original Latin form. Katharsis is an emotional state. It is the purification of emotions through art. It is when the person’s imagination and taste is on the same level as the work of art giving them pleasure and helping them feel peace of mind.

This defines the work of our studio. We create merely good, correct, and efficient works that correspond to the worldview and imagination of our clients. Besides the brand name, our logo has a big role as well when it comes to impressing people. It is easy to recognize and remember both consciously and subconsciously.

The logo has been created in a minimalistic style and has a great meaning behind it. It is a bridge mimicking the geometrical design of Armenian bridges. It represents an object connecting two dots. In this case it portrays the connection of the achievements in art and design and the unification of old and new styles making it clearer for the person to understand art.
