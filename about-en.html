WHAT IS GRAPHIC DESIGN
WHY IS IT IMPORTANT

Graphic design is a way of visual communication through typography, photography, and illustrations. It is a type of visual language that helps people to solve problems in a creative way. Graphic design has considerably developed for the past two decades. The demand for graphic designs has grown so much that it is almost impossible to find a business which doesn’t use graphic design or elements of graphic design. At present, graphic design is highly necessary and irreplaceable in every field. Graphic design helps businesses to earn more credibility among customers, it is an essential part of marketing, and increases the business profits.
